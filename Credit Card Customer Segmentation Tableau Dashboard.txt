Below is the link to my Tableau Public Profile for better experience.

The 5 important conclusions made from the above dashboard are mentioned as follows:

1. More tahn 50% of the Customer has done the repayment of the Minimum Credit Amount.
2. Credit Score of people with age range between 20-50 has a good Credit Score.
3. Credit Score of Media Manager lies in Good Category whereas for Entrepreneur lies in Poor Category.
4. The ratio of male and female customers is approximately 1:1.
5. There is less significance of the geography of the client.
